Fresh and Local

All our dishes and drinks are served fresh, and sourced form local, ethical suppliers. We aim to serve the highest quality of food, while being sustainable, green, healthy and absolutely delicious, which means you can enjoy all our dishes Guilt free !!

Social

We pride ourselves on our outstanding service and role in the local creative community. Almeria is a staple of the local area for its cultural and social events. We are frequented daily by creatives, expats and locals, our staff is very friendly and will ensure you enjoy your time with us.
