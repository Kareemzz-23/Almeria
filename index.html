Fresh and Local

All our dishes and drinks are served fresh, and sourced from local, ethical suppliers. We aim to serve the highest quality of food, while being sustainable, green, healthy and absolutely delicious, which means you can enjoy all our dishes guilt-free!

Atmosphere

At Almeria, we pride ourselves on our outstanding service and impact in the local creative community. Based in Downtown Cairo, Almeria's atmosphere is reminiscent of old Cairo life, full of traditional Egyptian chairs and traditional Egyptian food, which is why Almeria is daily frequented by creatives, expats, and locals, who wish to experience the true spirit of Egypt. Our establishment also holds many social and creative events for you to enjoy. Our staff are international, friendly, and love a good laugh, which means we always bring the good vibes!
