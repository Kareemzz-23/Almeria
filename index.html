Fresh and Local

All our dishes and drinks are served fresh, and sourced form local, ethical suppliers. We aim to serve the highest quality of food, while being sustainable, green, healthy and absolutely delicious, which means you can enjoy all our dishes Guilt free !!

Atmosphere

At Almeria, We pride ourselves on our outstanding service and impact in the local creative community. Based in Downtown Cairo, Almeria's atmosphere is reminscent of old cairo life, full with traditional Egyptian chairs and traditional Egyptian food, which is why Almeria is daily frequented by creatives, expats and locals, who wish to experience the true spirit of Egypt. Our establishment also holds many social and creative events for you to enjoy. Our staff are international, friendly and love a good laugh, which means we always bring the good vibes !!
